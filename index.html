Curriculum Vitae

Gabriele is currently a research economist at the Banca d'Italia. Prior to joining Banca d’Italia, he worked as an economist at the Bank of England in Financial Stability: first in the International Finance Division (2009-11) and then in the Risk Assessment Division (2011-13). Gabriele has also been a visiting scholar at the International Monetary Fund, Warwick Business School and Manchester Business School. He holds a PhD in Finance from the University of Tor Vergata in Rome.

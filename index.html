Curriculum Vitae

I am a research economist at the Banca d'Italia. Prior to joining Banca d’Italia, I worked as economist at the Bank of England in Financial Stability: first in the International Finance Division (2009-11) and then in the Risk Assessment Division (2011-13). I have also been visiting scholar at the International Monetary Fund. I hold a PhD in Finance from the University of Tor Vergata in Rome.
